Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 7, 2026

AuthToolkit provides temporary inboxes designed for development, testing, automation workflows, and privacy-conscious use. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use the service and how that information may be used.

What AuthToolkit does

AuthToolkit offers disposable email inboxes for short-term use cases such as signup testing, verification flow testing, QA, automation, and reducing exposure of a personal email address.

Information we may collect

  • Temporary inbox identifiers and generated email addresses
  • Email metadata and message content received by temporary inboxes
  • Technical information such as IP address, browser type, timestamps, and device details
  • Operational and security logs used for abuse prevention, rate limiting, and reliability

How information may be used

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the service
  • To deliver messages to temporary inboxes
  • To detect, investigate, and prevent spam, fraud, abuse, or misuse
  • To troubleshoot errors, measure reliability, and improve performance
  • To comply with legal obligations and valid requests

Temporary inboxes are not private or permanent storage

Temporary inboxes are built for convenience and testing, not for permanent or confidential communication. Messages may expire, may be deleted automatically, and should not be used for highly sensitive, financial, medical, legal, or other confidential information.

Retention

Inbox records, email content, and related logs may be retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for platform operation, service reliability, abuse review, security monitoring, and legal compliance. Temporary inboxes and their messages may be deleted automatically after expiration.

Cookies, local storage, and similar technologies

AuthToolkit may use local storage, cookies, or similar browser technologies to support core site functionality, maintain temporary session behavior, improve user experience, and support future analytics or advertising features.

Third-party infrastructure

AuthToolkit may rely on third-party providers for hosting, storage, message processing, analytics, logging, security, or related infrastructure. These providers may process limited data as needed to support the service.

Abuse prevention and compliance

Because disposable inboxes can be misused, AuthToolkit may monitor technical activity, apply rate limits, investigate abuse reports, and restrict or block access associated with harmful or prohibited behavior.

Your choices

If you do not want information associated with your temporary use of the service to be processed under this policy, do not use AuthToolkit. You should avoid using temporary inboxes for information you consider sensitive or important to retain.

Children

AuthToolkit is not intended for use by children under 13, and the service should not be used to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the platform evolves. The latest version posted on this page will apply.
