About AuthToolkit

Built for disposable inbox testing, privacy, and developer utility.

AuthToolkit is a privacy-first disposable inbox service designed for testing signups, verification flows, transactional messages, and development workflows without using a personal email address.

What we do

AuthToolkit provides temporary inboxes that help users receive email for short-term, low-risk workflows such as testing app behavior, reviewing email templates, checking verification delivery, and reducing inbox clutter.

The service is built with a simple goal: make temporary inbox access fast, clear, and privacy-conscious while keeping expectations honest about limitations, retention, and use.

Who it is for

AuthToolkit may be useful for:

  • Developers testing email verification, magic links, and password reset flows
  • QA teams validating transactional email behavior
  • Users who want to avoid sharing a personal inbox for low-value signups
  • Builders exploring privacy-focused tools for short-lived email workflows

What it is not for

AuthToolkit is not intended for unlawful activity, impersonation, fraud, harassment, abuse of third-party platforms, or bypassing a service’s terms, policies, or security controls.

Temporary inboxes also should not be relied on for sensitive, permanent, or high-value accounts because inboxes expire, content may be deleted, and continuity is not guaranteed.

Our approach

We prefer a straightforward product: fast temporary inbox creation, simple UI, clear limits, and transparent disclosures. As the platform grows, we may expand into additional developer tools around verification flows, sandbox environments, and privacy-oriented testing utilities.

Product direction

AuthToolkit is being developed as part of a broader toolkit vision focused on practical infrastructure for developers and privacy-conscious workflows. That includes areas such as:

  • Email and OTP testing tools
  • Webhook and API utilities
  • Verification sandbox workflows
  • Reusable infrastructure for future developer products
