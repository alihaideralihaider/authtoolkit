# Productization Scorecard

Score each category from 1 to 5.

| Category | 1 | 3 | 5 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Revenue potential | Low willingness to pay | Useful add-on | Clear paid product or strong upsell |
| Reuse across industries | One-off workflow | Works in adjacent verticals | Broadly reusable across many industries |
| Technical difficulty | Large unknowns | Moderate integration work | Simple, well-bounded MVP |
| Existing code overlap | Mostly new work | Some reusable code | Strong overlap with existing code |
| Messaging/API dependency | Requires fragile external dependency | Depends on standard provider APIs | Can work with current stack or clean adapter |
| Compliance risk | High regulatory or consent risk | Manageable review needed | Low risk with clear controls |
| Speed to MVP | More than 4 weeks | 1-3 weeks | A few days to 1 week |

## Recommended Interpretation

- 28-35: Strong candidate for near-term productization.
- 20-27: Worth prototyping after scope is narrowed.
- 14-19: Keep as research or internal-only tooling.
- 7-13: Do not productize yet.

For technical difficulty, messaging/API dependency, and compliance risk, score higher when the path is easier and safer.
