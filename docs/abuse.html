Abuse & Responsible Use

AuthToolkit provides temporary inboxes designed for development, testing, automation workflows, and privacy protection.

While the service is intended for legitimate use, we take abuse reports seriously and investigate misuse of our infrastructure.

Report Abuse

If you believe AuthToolkit temporary inboxes are being used for abusive or malicious activity, please contact:

abuse@authtoolkit.com

Please include

  • The temporary email address involved
  • Date and time of the activity
  • The service or platform affected
  • Any logs, screenshots, or relevant evidence

Prohibited Use

AuthToolkit services must not be used for activities including but not limited to:

  • Spam or bulk unsolicited messaging
  • Fraud or impersonation
  • Harassment or abuse
  • Illegal activity or attempts to bypass service protections

Enforcement

AuthToolkit may investigate reported activity and may restrict access, block domains, or take other actions necessary to prevent misuse of the platform.
